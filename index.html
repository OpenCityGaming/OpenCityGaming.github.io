Welcome to OpenCity Gaming.
We're a small, community driven team, working to bring an exciting and optimised brand-new Roleplay gamemode for Garry's Mod - all with an open-minded approach to contribution, collaboration and development.

We think it's important that everyone should be able to contribute and develop easily and transparently in a roleplay community, so we're making our gamemode almost entirely public to help contributors, and make our community thrive. In addition to this, we're bringing innovative ways for those who don't wish to code to be able to contribute too.
Join our Discord and be a part of our community, and go to our GitHub to start contributing!
